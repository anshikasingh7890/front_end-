my favourite book: "The Great Gatsy"

"The Great Gatsby" is a novel written by F.Scott Fitzgerald and was first published in 1925. It is widely consider one of the greatest American novels. The story is set during the Roaring Twentiesandexplores theme of love ,wealth,and the American Dream.

The novel's main charachter,jay Gatsby,is famous for his extravagant parties and unrequired love for Daisy Buchanan. His story ultimetly leads to tragic consequence.The book is known for its symbolism and its critical view of the American Dream.
